Windows Cleaner

A PowerShell script to uninstall all of Microsoft's built-in apps.

Sick of Windows 10 cluttering my desktop, I wrote Windows Cleaner: a Powershell script which removes all of Microsoft's built-in applications. There is less-than-no need for a Sports app on my workstation, and frankly, I feel as though I don't own my computer anymore. Auto-updates, data mining, etc... are all solid reasons why one should conduct their business on Linux, but sometimes, the need arises for a Windows-based machine.

Windows Cleaner removes the following apps: 3D Builder, Alarms & Clock, Calendar & Mail, Camera, Get Office, Get Skype, Get Started, Groove Music, Maps, Microsoft Solitaire Collection, Money, Movies & TV, News, OneNote, People, Phone Companion, Sports, Voice Recorder, Weather and Xbox.

You can optionally remove the following apps: Photos and Store.
