Libre Thoughts

A scrapbook of short stories, novellas and other types of written content.

Inspired by ZenPencils, I began creating a sort-of scrapbook for short stories (and other kinds of written content). I wanted to have a completely different stylesheet for each article item. I created a general style framework—which is applied to all article elements—as well as a separate stylesheet for every article.
