Laputan Machines

A WordPress theme for my personal video game blog.

I wanted to start a video game blog, so I decided that the first step would be to design and develop a WordPress theme. This theme is very much tailored to me and my preferences, but it can be adapted with some tweaking.

Posts are tagged as either 'editorials' or 'reviews.' Two pages display all articles tagged with their relevant tags. The landing page shows ten articles—one featured article (the most recently posted) and nine more listed in chronological order. Setting a featured thumbnail for each post is key as it is used for the article's display badge. Article excerpts are important too—they are shown when an article is in the featured position.

The site description and links list (on the landing page) can be changed through WordPress' widget manager. On single posts, the right sidebar can similarly be changed to include different widgets. At the moment, the right sidebar shows comments, three recent articles and tagged categories.
