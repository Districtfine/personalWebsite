About the Human

Michael like to make things. He started off making mods for The Sims 1/2, Deus Ex, and The Movies. Past-Michael's interests turned to caricature creation, then cartoon illustration. Afterwards, Past-Michael floated about, taking on anything which came to mind.
Michael has a habit of revisiting old, long-forgotten projects with the goal of redoing them—for some reason. Deus Ex: Timeline, for instance, was remade four times (Future-Michael may revisit it again). This site was remade ten times. Current-Michael doesn't know why this happens, but he's convinced that it's not entirely healthy.
