About the Human

Making stuff is fun. I started off making mods for The Sims 1 and 2, Deus Ex, and The Movies—to name a few. Past-Michael seemed to enjoy adding himself into simulation/management games (for some reason). I stopped to work on cartoon illustration (I'd kill to see those old panels again). From there, I did a bunch of different 'things.' Sometimes, I'd return to game modding, other times I'd get sucked into something completely tangential. I also have a bad habit of returning to old, long-forgotten projects and completely redoing them. Deus Ex: Timeline was remade about five times (I might do it again, depending on how Future-Michael is feeling), and this website went through ten-odd redesigns before it became this. I'm not entirely convinced that doing this is healthy, so I try to force myself to take on new projects whenever I get a craving for nostalgia. I blame the caffeine.
