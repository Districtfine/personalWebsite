The first in a series of three free-to-use website templates, Europa boasts a clean and succinct landing page, pretty content pages, and a crisp color scheme. The font, like the landing page's backdrop, is highly geometric, and the showcase cards lead to dedicated showcase pages. Button and showcase links are all designed to play off of one another visually—your project's colors provide the accent.
